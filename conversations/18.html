18.
The hills
I'm fed up. I'm bored, actually, with Melbourne.
But Melbourne's a great city! The gardens, the Yarra, the cafés ...
But I never see them. All I see is the 903 bus. When I go home, I'll say 'Warrigal Road's nice'.
Oh, come on. That's Monday to Friday. What about the weekends?
What about them? I'm in my room, skyping friends in other countries.
That's silly. Let's do something. I know! We'll take the train to Belgrave and then get on Puffing Billy.
Puffing Billy?
It's an old steam train. It goes around the hills. We'll get off at Emerald Lake and spend a couple of hours there.
Sounds good! Sounds better than the 903 bus!
