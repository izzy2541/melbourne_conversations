Where do you work?
I'm working at a restaurant in the city. It's in Swanston St and it's called The Red Pony.
What hours do you work?
Well, generally I start at six o'clock and work till midnight but sometimes the customers stay and I don't get out of there till one o'clock, or even later.
Do you like it there?
The only problem is the boss sometimes gets really angry if any of us stuff up. Like the other night the customer complained that the bill was too expensive and that we'd got it wrong. So, the boss hit the roof even though it was only a few dollars difference.
What did he do?
Well, he looked like he was going to explode but we sorted it out. In the end we all stayed back and had a meal together.
Glad to hear it ended well.
